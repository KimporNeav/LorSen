Our Product

LorSen is a software as a service that aims to assist our customers who are cloth retailers and already have their own application to gain and build trust with their customers by allowing them to try on the clothes virtually.

Argumented Reality

AR is a technology that superimposes virtual elements onto the real world. It uses devices like smartphones and headsets with cameras, sensors, and software to recognize and track real-world objects and add virtual information on top of them.

AR is different from Virtual Reality (VR) which creates a completely artificial environment, while AR adds virtual elements to the real world.

About Us

Vision

Our vision at LorSen is to transform the cloth retail industry by revolutionizing the way customers experience and interact with clothing online. We strive to be the leading provider of virtual try-on solutions, empowering retailers to build trust, enhance customer engagement, and drive business growth through immersive digital experiences.

Mission

At LorSen, we empower cloth retailers with personalized and realistic virtual try-on technology. Our mission is to boost customer confidence and trust by enabling them to visualize how clothes will look and fit before purchasing. Through innovation, customization, and a commitment to privacy and security, we support retailers in achieving sustainable success.
